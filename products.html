    Single hung

    Only one pane moves.
    The top panel can move down to protect a fall or a swimming pool area. The bottom panel can slide up to form a servery or in tall windows with a high sill to allow for mid level ventilation.

    Standard

    Often used to protect a fall or a pool area, providing non-protuding high level ventilation.

    Bottom Sliding

    A bottom sliding shugg for counter top serveries or high level windows.

    Custom Design

    Many combinations can be produced to best suit your individual requirements.

    Custom Shape

    Shuggs can be customised to work with different shapes and styles.

    Double hung

    Both panes move at the same time. When fully open the glass is in the middle with air flowing through the top and bottom openings. The most economical Shugg.

    Standard

    The original Shugg is the most economical solution and the high and low ventilation is the most effective.

    Three Pane

    An effective, economical solution for tall openings. Many different combinations are possible.

    Four Pane

    Often used in cafes or restaurants. Applications for mid level ventilation. Best for tall and wide openings.

    Custom Shape

    Shuggs can be customised to work with different shapes and styles.

    Cavity Slider

    A single hung panel can be made to slide into a cavity. Ideal for a servery situation or even use a Shugg as a door.

    Standard

    Often used in office receptions or ticketing booths. Window slides into a cavity above.

    Bottom Sliding

    Similar usage except window slides into a cavity below.

    Custom Design

    Many options can be created to customise these windows to best suit your individual requirements.

    Super Shugg

    Our Super Shugg commercial units can move huge sheets of glass. They are a favourite with bars, restaurants and cafes wanting that indoor/outdoor feel.
    Our installation team often remove large fixed panes and install Super Shuggs into the existing frame to provide welcome ventilation.
