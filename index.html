SERVICES

Flexibility

The Shugg vertical sliding system offers a variety of shapes, sizes and finishes to complement any architectural design. Shugg units can have curved raking or arched heads with power coated, anodised or timber finishes and are available in manual and motorised versions.

Design

The Shugg system are adaptable to any situation. Our design team will work with you and provide drawings to ensure the ideal solution for you specific situation. Shuggs are suitable for all glass types including double glazing, high performance Low E or even solid panels.

Installation

Our Shugg team will work anywhere throughout New Zealand or even the world. Kit sets are available with comprehensive instructions for remote or overseas locations to enable local glass purchase and assembly.
